Frequently Asked Questions

Will resizing distort my images?
No. Pixiva's resizer uses scale percentages which adjust width and height proportionally. This locks the original aspect ratio and prevents any warping or stretching.
How private are my images?
Your privacy is absolute. Processing takes place locally in your browser memory. We never transmit your images to any remote servers or APIs.
